About Us

Welcome to Casa Selera in Râșnov

Experience genuine hospitality and a peaceful retreat at Casa Selera. Nestled in the charming town of Râșnov, our guesthouse offers the perfect blend of comfort and convenience, just a short walk from Dino Parc and near the historic Rasnov Citadel.

Whether you're here to explore the local attractions like Bran Castle or simply unwind, we strive to make your visit memorable. Enjoy our cozy rooms, friendly service, and the beautiful surroundings.
